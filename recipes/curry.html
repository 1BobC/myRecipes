Curry

A simple curry side dish

Description

This simple side dish of curried potato and cauliflower
is a welcome addition to any curry meal.

Ingredients enough for a helping each for four people.

  • One large potato
  • Five or six cauliflower florets
  • Small onion or shallot
  • 1/2 tsp Korma spice powder
  • 1/2 tsp Tumeric spice powder
  • 1 tbsp Cooking oil
  • A few fresh coriander leaves to garnish

Steps

  1. Slice potato into 2cm cubes and steam with cauliflower florets.
  2. Slice onion / shallot.
  3. When potato and cauliflower have steamed but are still firm,
    heat the cooking oil in a small frying pan.
  4. When cooking oil is hot add the onion / shallot and allow
    to soften, then add both spices.
  5. If too dry add a tspn of water and let it warm up.
  6. Stir the mixture and add the cubed potato and cauliflower.
  7. When it is cooked through, serve with a garnish of coriander leaves.

It smells and tastes great!
