Pasta

A filling pasta dish.

Description

An ultra-simple tasty and filling dish for lunch or dinner on your own.

Ingredients for a singleton.

  • 75 gm Spaghetti
  • 1 tbspn pesto of choice (I like kale and garlic!).

Steps

  1. Boil one litre of water.
  2. When water is boiling carefully add the spaghetti.
  3. Let the spaghetti cook to your liking (I like it a bit al dente)
  4. Drain, serve in a warm dish and stir in the pesto.

Voila! a tasty and filling dish.
